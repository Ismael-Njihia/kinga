About Us

Personal care for your healthy living

Kinga Ceragem Limited, established in 2016 in Kenya, aims to enhance human life through innovative spinal therapeutic appliances, dietary supplements, and business consultancy services, focusing on protecting and promoting the health and interests of individuals and corporations globally.

Our Services

First Time Consultation

The process includes new customer registration with health protocol adherence, consultation on healthcare history aligning with Kinga Ceragem & Alternative Medicine, and guidance for maximizing customer benefits moving forward.

Daily Walk-in Customer

Customers are advised to use Kinga Ceragem thermo-therapeutic devices for at least two 2-hour sessions daily, with a 1-hour daily session at the center also proving effective; waiting time should not exceed 30 minutes due to traffic.

Online Reservation Customer

Similar to daily work in but no waiting since its express service for extremely busy walk-in clients

2-week Kinga@ Home Special Session

The product is designed for convenient home use, accessible to family, friends, and relatives for three or more daily sessions, complemented by personalized healthcare management services offered by Kinga Healthcare Consultants

Home device experiential service

We provide Kinga Home services where customers can rent our thermo-acupressure devices for two weeks or a month at a reasonable fee, delivered to their premises with guidance from a healthcare consultant

Outdoor service for Corporates and Institutions

We Partner with Corporates and institutions to offer our health care management services for the benefit of their key stakeholders such as employees/staff, customers and their other valued stakeholders.
